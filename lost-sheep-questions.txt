Lost Sheep Game — 18 Questions

Based on Luke 15:1-7

============================================================
 1. Who came to hear Jesus?
    A) Tax collectors   B) Grumpy leaders
    Correct: A

 2. How many sheep did the man have?
    A) Ten sheep   B) One hundred sheep
    Correct: B

 3. How many sheep got lost?
    A) One sheep   B) Ten sheep
    Correct: A

 4. Where did the man leave the ninety-nine?
    A) The open field   B) A locked barn
    Correct: A

 5. Did the shepherd care about the sheep?
    A) Yes   B) No
    Correct: A

 6. Did the man give up looking?
    A) No, he searched   B) Yes he quit
    Correct: A

 7. How did he carry the found sheep?
    A) On his shoulders   B) In a wagon
    Correct: A

 8. How did the man feel when he found it?
    A) Very happy   B) Very mad
    Correct: A

 9. Where did he take the sheep?
    A) Back home   B) The dark woods
    Correct: A

10. Who did the man call together?
    A) Friends and neighbors   B) Nobody at all
    Correct: A

11. What did he say to his friends?
    A) Rejoice with me   B) Please go away
    Correct: A

12. How many sheep did NOT need help?
    A) Ninety-nine   B) Only five
    Correct: A

13. What happens in heaven when someone comes back to God?
    A) There is joy   B) Nothing at all
    Correct: A

14. Who is the shepherd like?
    A) God   B) A bad king
    Correct: A

15. Who is the lost sheep like?
    A) A lost sinner   B) Perfect people
    Correct: A

16. Who was grumpy at Jesus?
    A) The grumpy leaders   B) The tax collectors
    Correct: A

17. Why did Jesus tell this story?
    A) God loves us   B) To scare people
    Correct: A

18. Is God happy when we come back to him?
    A) Yes, very happy   B) No, not really
    Correct: A
============================================================
